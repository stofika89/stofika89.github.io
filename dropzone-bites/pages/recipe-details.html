Avocado Chicken Wrap

15 minutes 350 kcal 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 large chicken breast
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 whole wheat tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 2 tbsp salsa

Instructions

  1. Grill the chicken breast until fully cooked, then slice it into strips.
  2. Mash the avocado in a bowl and spread it onto each tortilla.
  3. Layer the sliced chicken, shredded lettuce, cheese, and salsa on the tortillas.
  4. Roll up the tortillas tightly, then cut them in half.
