Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how DropZone Bites collects, uses, and protects the personal information of users who interact with the platform.

2. What Data We Collect

When you register, we collect your email address. Optionally, you may upload a display name or profile image. When submitting content (such as recipes or logbook entries), the information you provide is stored in your user account.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to manage your account and to display your content to other users. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Cookies and Analytics

DropZone Bites may use basic cookies to enhance user experience (e.g. language preference). We do not use third-party tracking or analytics tools.

5. Data Security

Your account is protected via secure authentication. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. If you wish to delete your account and associated content, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Major changes will be announced on the platform.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or requests regarding this policy, please contact us at: email@email.com
